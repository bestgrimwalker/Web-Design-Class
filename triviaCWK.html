Ice cream cone was an accident

In 1904, at the St. Louis World's Fair, an ice cream vendor ran out of bowls to serve his ice cream. A neighboring waffle vendor came to the rescue by rolling up waffles to create edible cones for the ice cream. This happy accident led to the creation of the ice cream cone as we know it today!
